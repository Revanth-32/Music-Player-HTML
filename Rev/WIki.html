Wikipedia[c] is a free content online encyclopedia written and maintained by a community of volunteers, known a Wikipedians, through open collaboration and the wiki software MediaWiki. Wikipedia is the largest and most-read reference work in history,[3][4] and is consistently ranked among the ten most visited websites; as of April 2024, it was ranked fourth by Semrush,[5] and seventh by Similarweb.[6] Founded by Jimmy Wales and Larry Sanger on January 15, 2001, Wikipedia has been hosted since 2003 by the Wikimedia Foundation,an American nonprofit organization funded mainly by donations from readers.Initially only available in English, editions of Wikipedia in more than 300 other languages have been developed.
